About Us

We’re a group of friends —Wandy(Photographer), Thando (Software devloper) and Thando(stylist), — who started this as students in a business school that pushed a strict formal dress code. But we weren’t about to let that limit our expression. Instead of sticking to the usual suit-and-tie look, we created our own version of formal — bold, stylish, and unapologetically us.

This is more than a fashion project. It’s a blueprint. A collective. A celebration of individuality in a world that keeps trying to dress us the same.
