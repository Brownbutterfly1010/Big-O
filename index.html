About Us

We’re a group of friends —Wandy Ndlovu, Thando Shongwe and Thando Ntshalintshail, — who started this as students in a business school that pushed a strict formal dress code. But we weren’t about to let that limit our expression. Instead of sticking to the usual suit-and-tie look, we created our own version of formal — bold, stylish, and unapologetically us.

One of us builds in code. One of us sees through the lens. One of us hears the world in melodies. Different skills, different backgrounds, different ways of expressing ourselves — but somehow, it clicks.

This is more than a fashion project. It’s a blueprint. A collective. A celebration of individuality in a world that keeps trying to dress us the same.

What’s Next

We will continue styling the world and exchanging collective ideas, blending creativity with individuality to redefine formal fashion. Our journey is about pushing boundaries, inspiring confidence, and building a community where everyone can express their unique style boldly and authentically. Together with our friends and collaborators, we aim to transform the way people perceive formal wear — making it vibrant, inclusive, and truly personal. This is just the beginning, and we’re excited to grow, innovate, and shape the future of fashion, one look at a time.
